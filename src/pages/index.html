Remove My Porn is now Incognito Bear

Removemyporn.com was a popular website that offered users the ability to remove NSFW (Not Safe For Work) websites and content from their browsing history.

However, the website has been rebranded as Incognito Bear, a browser extension for Chrome and Edge. With just one click, Incognito Bear removes any NSFW websites and content from your browsing history, so you can enjoy worry-free browsing.

The internet can be a dangerous place, and it's easy to stumble upon inappropriate content accidentally. This can be embarrassing, especially if you're using a computer that others have access to.

Incognito Bear helps to prevent these types of situations by automatically removing NSFW content from your browsing history. This means that even if someone else uses your computer, they won't be able to see what you've been looking at.

Incognito Bear is also useful for people who work in industries that require them to use computers that are shared with others. For example, healthcare workers, teachers, and government employees often use shared computers. With Incognito Bear, they can browse the internet without worrying about accidentally stumbling upon inappropriate content.

Downloading Incognito Bear is easy and takes just a few minutes. The extension is available for both Chrome and Edge and can be downloaded from the Chrome Web Store or the Microsoft Edge Add-ons Store. Once installed, the extension runs in the background and automatically removes any NSFW content from your browsing history.

Wrapping up

In conclusion, Incognito Bear is a useful tool for anyone who wants to browse the internet without worrying about accidentally stumbling upon inappropriate content. Whether you're using a shared computer or just want to keep your browsing history private, Incognito Bear is a must-have browser extension. So, go ahead and download it today!
